## Post 1 18th March 2026

Copy-pasting into one chat box sucks.

So we built a canvas for LLMs, content, and research.

Add markdown docs, paste social links, upload files, and connect to AI chats.

Agents can handle busy work, but creative work needs a visual space to connect ideas.

Canvas is just one feature in Eden, though.

Eden is a mix between a drive and second brain (because why don't second brain apps support video?? doesn't make sense as a creator) that transcribes and analyzes anything you upload, including every video frame (which is very helpful when trying to find that specific shot, especially when referencing in a chat).

We want Eden to be the workspace for your entire creative workflow.

Custom agents will be a thing soon, but for now, Eden is the place to write, research, and finally have everything in one place.
